Our Story

A True Definition of Luxury

Urban Bliss Hotel was established in 2019 to reward a good life by providing guests with a unique hospitality experience. Located in Kabwe, Zambia, the hotel has become known as an oasis of peace and tranquility, combining luxury accommodation, excellent dining, wellness services, and exceptional customer care.

We believe every guest deserves to feel cherished, relaxed, and inspired — and our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every stay exceeds expectation.
